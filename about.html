About DrawTurings

DrawTurings is a platform dedicated to drawing and simulating Turing machines using a simple grahical interface. You are able to create Turing machines from scratch, run them step-by-step or at full speed, and visualize the tape and state transitions in real-time.

Key Features

  • Intuitive Design Interface: DrawTurings offers an easy-to-use graphical interface to design Turing machines.
  • Simulation and Visualization: Run your Turing machines step-by-step or at full speed, and visualize the tape and state transitions in real-time.
  • Sharing of Turing Machines : Share your Turing machine designs with yourself or others by exporting and importing them as JSON files.

About me

I developed DrawTurings as a personal project after realizing there was no platform available that allowed me to design Turing Machines using a graphical interface. I am not an expert, so I welcome any corrections or feedback you may have.
